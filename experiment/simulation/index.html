flakiness index

STEP  1 

Objective:

To determine the flakiness index of the coarse aggregate.


Apparatus used:

Apparatus used: Flakiness index gauge, sieve shaker, coarse aggregates, sieves of size 25mm,20mm, 16mm, 12.5mm, 10mm and a Pan.



STEP  2 
 Place an empty tray on the weighing balance and TARE the balance. Put about 200 pieces of aggregate in the tray and weigh them.  

STEP  3 
  Arrange the sieves in descending order, with the largest aperture size at the top and the smallest one at the bottom and place a pan below the lower most sieve.  

STEP  4 
 Now, put the weighed aggregates on the sieve stack. 

STEP  5 
  Cover the sieve stack with the lid and place it on the sieve shaker. Start the sieve shaker, and after some time, remove the sieves and record the aggregates collected in each sieve.  

STEP  6 
  Now, pass the aggregates through 40-25mm slot of the flakiness gauge. Keep the passed and the retained aggregates separately.
